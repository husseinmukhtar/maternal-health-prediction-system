Defense Explanation

The project uses unsupervised learning because the K-Means model detects hidden groupings from maternal health measurements without depending on labelled outcomes during training. The clusters are later interpreted as low, medium, and high risk groups by comparing the cluster centers and clinical risk tendencies.
