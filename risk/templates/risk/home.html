How the system works

The system collects maternal clinical indicators such as age, blood pressure, blood sugar, body temperature, heart rate, and gestational age. These values are scaled and passed into a K-Means clustering model. The clusters are interpreted as risk groups based on the clinical pattern of the cluster centers.
